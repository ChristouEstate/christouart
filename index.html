Christou Estate

The work of Alexandra Christou is managed by the Christou Estate, a privately held artist estate. The estate preserves, contextualises and advances the artist’s oeuvre through exhibitions, publications and collaborations.

All copyright and reproduction matters are administered by the Christou Estate.
