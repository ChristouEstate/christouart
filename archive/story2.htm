Outside there were lots of cats. They don't have homes and live in the streets.... I had to go out immediately and meet everybody. I wanted to, but everybody was scared of me and ran away!. Alexandra took me for a walk to the old Castro, on top of the hill, above the house...She says it's Venetian.! Well, I don't know what that is, but I love it up there. There is a lot of open space where I can race around and jump and smell everything.... A lot of old houses I can look in and from everywhere I can see the sea below... It's really beautiful!. Some times there is a goat in the Castro, eating the flowers. There are a lot of flowers up there in the spring. Well, I love goats too, would like to play with them, but they are very afraid of me and run away. They run very, very fast.

So we lived on the island and were very happy.! She combed my hair, put on my scarf with the blue bead, and we went out for walks around the old village, through the narrow streets. Then we went to the Cafenion for an ouzo! They let me sit on a chair and gave me nice things to eat! We met lots of people. I liked the people and they liked me too, after they got to know me, that is, and were sure I wouldn't bite them.

They said I was a cartoon! I don't know what that is, but it sounded o.k.

Alexandra was painting. She painted me too, in some of her pictures. She is crazy about me and we are always, always together!. I always sat with her, when she was working, or slept there. Then we would go swimming. I would race on the beach, jump in the water, roll on my back and play .. I love going swimming!. When I come out I always dig a hole and lay in it. Alexandra covers me with a towel and I sleep, nice and cool. We have a very nice life!
