Alexandra and John were very busy and made Ceramics....the whole winter long...!. They are both very mad about me, I mean they really love me... so John made little heads that look like me and put them on the pots that Alexandra made on a thing that turns, and they call a "wheel"!! . Then, they would put everything in the "kiln" and turn it on.... It burned for a long time, and it was great, because it made the house very warm for a couple of days...! Then the Ceramics came out and everybody was very, but very excited!!

They call the things, the "Ceramics" I mean, ROONIWARE! This is me, you understand. They are so mad about me, they love me so much, they named everything after me, ROONI!!
